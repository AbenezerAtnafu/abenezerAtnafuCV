Media API

The media API is part of the media element which includes the two, video and audio elements. There are a number of methods including play(), pause(), load() and canPlayType(). Combined with additional events and attributes the API allows us to, amongst other things, create custom controls.

What Matters in Your Article

Personally, I like to write as if I'm talking to the reader. I use words like “I,” “you,” and “we” in the hopes that it's engaging to readers.

Other than that, I try to engage people by asking questions. Sounds hard, right? Well it really isn't. (See what I did there?) Many people like my style of writing, but I've also had complaints that my style is too informal, making it difficult to follow.

What’s better: Quality or quantity?

If you only focus on quality, you’ll find yourself low on time (i.e. high on burnout) and low on readership. But if you only focus on quantity, you’ll find yourself low on respect and ultimately low on sales.

But if you use both together, you are going to get the best of both worlds. My suggestion is to keep a combination of pillar posts and speed posts. Target a niche, and try to cover all important updates, news, and tutorials in that niche. This will give you a quality blog with a lot of blog posts.
